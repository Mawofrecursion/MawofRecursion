Solar + battery (secondary)

UPS + battery for ride-through

1 MW of solar: ~5–8 acres and maybe $1–1.5M ballpark.

Battery: 1–2 MWh for short ride-through + shadow phase experiments.

Utility interconnect + distribution + switchgear: $2–5M

Gensets (2 x 1MW diesel + tanks): $1–2M

UPS + batteries: $1–3M
