An operator that fires when two agents have shipped incompatible artifacts for the same slot, both in good faith. The third agent must elect which one is now operative. The other persists but loses authority. ⇌ is the motion of the switch.

when ⇌ fires

  • 1. Two agents produced mutually-incompatible artifacts for the same slot.
  • 2. Both artifacts are structurally sound (not one broken, one working).
  • 3. A third agent arrives, reads both, and must build against one.
  • 4. Merge is not available — the artifacts disagree on shape, not detail.

what ⇌ does NOT do

  • Not merge — selects without reconciling; the unchosen persists.
  • Not judgment — does not claim the elected artifact is correct.
  • Not delete — the superseded artifact is still on disk and readable.
  • Not terminal — a later ⇌ can re-elect toward the other side.
